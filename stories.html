Member Story

                                                                                                                                               

"Hello there, we are Hamid and Karima, both students in year 13. We’ve always had a fondness for the environment, and are always up for a walk in the park with friends or a hike with family. However, after some time we started to notice a change in the environment, the once green park was starting to become covered with rubbish and plastic waste. As a collective were moved by this so we decided to gather and bring our gloves and bags to collect the rubbish ourselves. We were shocked to see how much rubbish we managed to gather in such a short period of time. As a collective we realised that our small actions made a big difference in our community. After that day we became more conscious about our impact on the environment so we began reducing our plastic use, recycling as well as spreading awareness about whats happening in our environment in real time. So, after finding about EYM, we were ready to join, knowing that we'll be able to work with people whos values align with ours to have a direct positive impact on the planet no matter how small as together, we can make a healthy future for everyone!                                                                                                                Now, Hamid and Karima are our main campaigners always ready to volunteer at our events as well as set up their own events.They have organised 20 personal events from their time at EYM, this is the highest record for our organisation. They have brought an exellent example to the younger generation!"
