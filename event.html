Beach Clean-Up Day

Date: 15th July

Location: Brighton Beach

What are we going to do?:Come and join us for an enjoyable day out at the beach, working together to clean up the environment. We ask our volunteers to work in pairs to collect the trash at the beach, using gloves and bags provided by the event organisers. So don’t worry about having to bring anything, JUST BRING YOUR ENERGY. It’s an excellent opportunity to have fun, breathe some fresh air, and make a difference to our environment that we all share. During the event, you’ll have the opportunity to make new friends, work in teams, and feel good about the good deed that you have accomplished. The pair collecting the most rubbish at the end of the session wins a surprise!
