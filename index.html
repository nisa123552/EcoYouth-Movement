About Us

Eco Youth Movement is a youth led campaign founded in 2023. Through the use of education, action and teamwork we work to build a greener future and show the youth that it is cool to care for the earth. Our ‘action’ consists of holding talks, cleaning parks, running marathons and much more. We are a strong community consisting of 60 members and hoping for more(make sure to join us today)!

Our Aims

  • Inspire youth to protect the environment
  • Raise awareness on the rapid changes in environment
  • Encourage youth activism

Activities

  • Beach clean-ups
  • Environmental talks
  • Tree planting
